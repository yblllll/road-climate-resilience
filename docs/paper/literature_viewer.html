Knowledge Gaps Identified

  • 1. Open Platform Gap (Theme E): No study provides a reproducible, open-source platform integrating weather, traffic, and network data. Your paper fills this gap!
  • 2. UK-Specific Empirical Gap: Most precipitation-speed studies use non-UK data. Your paper provides UK SRN evidence!
  • 3. Functional Form Consensus: Linear vs exponential vs threshold — no consensus. Gamma GLM offers a principled alternative!
  • 4. Multi-Factor Integration: Only Liu et al. (2026) integrates multiple rainfall impact pathways.
  • 5. Temporal Resolution: Most studies aggregate to hourly/daily. Sub-hourly analysis is rare.
  • 6. Recovery Dynamics: Few studies measure how quickly speeds return after rainfall cessation.
  • 7. Local Road Gap: Most resilience studies focus on highways and strategic roads.
  • 8. Validation Gap: Cross-validation between simulation and empirical under same conditions is almost absent.
